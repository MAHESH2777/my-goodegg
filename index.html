BUREAU VERITAS CERTIFIED

Consumers today are increasingly discriminating about the food they eat. Concerns about the ecological and health consequences of industrial and agricultural practices are fueling a consumer trend towards non-GMO and organic foods. The incidence of food allergies is on the rise, making proper food allergen labeling essential. Quality labels are keys to differentiating and demonstrating the premium quality of the products.
Bureau Veritas food product certification, testing and auditing solutions support food industry players like Good Eggs in addressing these challenges. From PDO, PGI and Label Rouge, to non-GMO, gluten-free and allergen labels, the food product certification from Bureau Veritas has enhanced our brand image and market acces

A mark of quality:

The Label Rouge mark only attests to the superior taste, quality and appearance of meat, poultry, seafood, dairy, fruit and other agricultural products and Good Eggs is proud to be certified by this mark. Label Rouge certified products must meet rigorous standards set by the French Ministry of Agriculture and we have been able to achieve the laurels by maintaining the most superior standards in the form of sustainable farming. Label Rouge certification has enabled us to add value to our products while assuring the performance of our organization’s quality management.

Shape a world of trust with Good Eggs and Bureau Veritas, the leading certification body!!
