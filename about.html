NUTRITIONAL
ENGINEERING

We are family-owned second-generation poultry farmers located in the suburbs of Hyderabad city. Since we are located quite close to the city, our supply of Eggs is always punctual and of fresh quality. The more closer, the fresher the eggs would be, right? We have our farms at different locations so that the supply of our eggs would be uninterrupted. Our 30 years of experience has helped us understand the nutritional engineering of the hen. We look after our hens like no other whilst keeping an eye on the environment and sustainability. Most of the electricity consumed on our farm is generated by the solar and biogas plant. The biogas is generated on-site from the manure produced by the hens.

VISION

Our vision is to move to a completely self-sustainable and eco-friendly farm very soon. We aim to incorporate the idea of living with nature in our every step.

MISSION

We have a complete 360 degree holistic understanding of farming and poultry. Our mission is to take poultry farming to the next level in Telangana! We comply with stringent health and safety regulations and as a result, produce salmonella-free eggs. We aim to produce a great quality Good Eggs where you have absolute confidence in our free-range farming and we meet our commitments to you, our customer.

GOALS

Our Sustainable farming integrates three main goals - environmental health, social and economic equity, and economic profitability.


The heritage of our family farm and the importance of both land and livestock have allowed us to take the lead in the development of sustainable practices as a core principle of the poultry business. A variety of philosophies, policies, and practices have contributed to this goal. People in many different capacities, from farmers to consumers, have shared this vision and contributed to it.

Last but not the least, Good Eggs is certified by Bureau Veritas, established in 1828 and a world leader in Testing, Inspection, and Certification. Anyone can name themselves but the proof ultimately speaks out the loudest. Isn’t it? We just don’t print the facts on our packages. The quality reflects in our Good Eggs too!!!

Our Holistic approaches focus on how the health of animals is integrated with the health of the environment and people. We maintain Bird health with practices that prevent the introduction of diseases, such as biosecurity. Increasingly, we raise birds without the routine use of antibiotics. We follow the Animal and Bird welfare guidelines to ensure the physical and mental well-being of our Good Hens!
