We are a family-owned second-generation poultry farmers located in the distant suburbs of Hyderabad city. Our 30 years of experience has helped us understand the nutritional engineering of hen. We look after our hens like no other whilst keeping an eye on the environment and sustainability. Most of the electricity consumed in our farm is generated by the solar and biogas plant. The biogas is generated on site from the manure produced by the hens. Our vision is to move to a completely self-sustainable and eco-friendly farm very soon. We aim to incorporate the idea of living with nature in our every step.

VISION

We have a complete 360 degree holistic understanding on farming and poultry. Our mission is to take the poultry farming to the next level in Telangana!

MISSION

Our Sustainable farming integrates three main goals - environmental health, social and economic equity, and economic profitability. The heritage of our family farm and the importance of both land and livestock have allowed us to take the lead in the development of sustainable practices as a core principle of the poultry business. A variety of philosophies, policies and practices have contributed to this goal. People in many different capacities, from farmers to consumers, have shared this vision and contributed to it.

GOALS

Our Holistic approaches focus on how the health of animals is integrated with the health of the environment and people. We maintain Bird health with practices that prevent the introduction of diseases, such as biosecurity and vaccination. Increasingly, we raise birds without the routine use of antibiotics. Medically-important antibiotics are only used under the supervision of a veterinarian to treat diseases. We follow the Animal and Bird welfare guidelines to ensure the physical and mental well-being of our Good Hens!
